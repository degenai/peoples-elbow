Usage

A few specifics

The marks are built to flex. These are the things that actually keep Elbow Room looking like Elbow Room.

Full color on light, gold on dark

The spine and the "Room" half of the wordmark are navy, so they disappear on a dark background. On navy, black, or a photo, use the gold emblem instead of the full-color lockup.

The emblem stands alone, the wordmark does not

The emblem carries the brand solo as an avatar, favicon, or watermark. The words "Elbow Room" should travel with the emblem or as the full lockup, not get reset in another typeface.

Give it the ring's worth of room

Leave clear space around the lockup roughly equal to the thickness of the emblem's outer ring. Crowd it against other elements and it stops reading as one mark.

The palette does the recognizing

Navy, gold, and sage are the brand. The one-color version (coming soon) is the only sanctioned recolor, and only for single-color print where the full palette cannot run.
