Personal Wiki Starter Kit

Build a personal knowledge graph with Claude and Obsidian. No coding required.

What's In The Kit

A minimal folder of markdown files that Claude maintains for you. You talk, Claude writes it down as interconnected wiki pages. Over time it becomes a map of your life: people, places, projects, ideas — all linked together in a graph you can browse in Obsidian.

Everything is local files on your computer. Nothing gets uploaded. You own it.

The kit is bare bones on purpose. The structure, the procedures, the workflow — all of it grows around you. Your wiki won't look like anyone else's.

How It Works

1

Talk

Just talk to Claude. About your family, a project, an idea, a memory. Claude captures what surfaces as wiki pages.

2

Interview

Say "interview me" and Claude asks warm, specific questions. Every answer becomes linked pages. 30 minutes can produce 10-20 pages.

3

Browse

Open the wiki folder in Obsidian. See your knowledge as an interactive graph. Click through connections. Watch it grow.
