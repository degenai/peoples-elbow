Our Mission

Justice. Relief. One elbow at a time.

The People's Elbow stands ready to defend your community against the Forces of Tension! We're not just another massage service - we're a mutual aid initiative bringing care directly to card shops, community markets, and spaces where people gather.

With our 50/50 split model, we ensure that both the massage therapist and host venues benefit equally. When you join the Convention of Tension Prevention, you become part of a movement dedicated to community wellness. No upselling, no data collection, just pure community-focused care.
