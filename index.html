Our Mission

We don't heal you. We empower you to rest.

The People's Elbow is not just another massage service. We're a mutual aid initiative bringing care directly to card shops, community markets, and spaces where people gather.

With a 50/50 split model, we ensure that both the massage therapist and host venues benefit equally. No upselling, no data collection, just pure community-focused care.

No Mission Creep

We amplify local causes without imposing our own agenda.

Mutual Aid over Branding

We serve communities without collecting emails or upselling packages.

Kayfabe with Integrity

Our superhero persona makes care approachable with sincere intentions.
