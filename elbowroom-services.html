The Work

Modalities, plainly described

Most sessions blend a few of these. We assess first, then use whatever the tissue actually calls for.

Deep Tissue

Slow, deliberate pressure into the deeper layers of muscle and fascia. For chronic tension, dense restriction, and the aches that do not let go.

Trigger Point

Precise, sustained pressure on the tight bands that refer pain elsewhere. The targeted answer to a knot that keeps lighting up the same spot.

Myofascial Release

Gentle, lengthening work through the connective tissue that wraps muscle. Restores glide and range when everything feels stuck together.

Sports & Recovery

Focused work for training loads and competition: tight hips, overworked shoulders, and the legs that carry the volume. Pairs well with cold plunge and sauna.

Relaxation

Smoother, flowing strokes for the days when the nervous system needs to come down more than a knot needs digging out. Still a full, unhurried hour.

Built Around You

Not sure which one you need? You do not have to. Tell us where it hurts, and the session is shaped from there. No menu memorization required.

What To Expect

Same shape, every session

Whether it is your first table session or your fortieth, the rhythm is the same: understand the problem, work it, and leave you with a plan.

1

The intake

A few minutes up front to learn what hurts, how it started, and what you want out of the hour. Old injuries, training goals, desk posture, all of it informs where the time goes.

2

The work

The clock starts when the hands do. Sixty minutes means sixty minutes of treatment, focused on the areas that need it, with pressure dialed to what your body wants that day.

3

The aftercare plan

You leave with a simple plan for the days after: what to stretch, what to ice or heat, what to ease off. No package math, no pressure to rebook. You come back when it helps.

Good To Know

Booking & policies

The fine print, without the fine print energy.

How do I pay?
Payment runs through Square at the time of service. You book your time online, and you settle up at the appointment. No memberships, no stored packages, no surprise charges.
What is the cancellation policy?
Please give at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Life happens, and we would rather give your slot to someone who needs it than charge you for it.
What should I wear?
For table sessions, you undress to your comfort level and stay draped throughout; only the area being worked is uncovered. For chair and on-site sessions, you stay fully clothed, so comfortable, loose layers work best.
Is the pricing final?
Rates on this page are preview pricing and may change. Table and chair rates are flat and posted up front. Recovery pairings and team or event work are quoted per engagement, with the number agreed before anything is booked.
Do you really not upsell?
Correct. You will not be pitched add-ons mid-session or pushed into a package at checkout. If a longer or more frequent session would genuinely help, we will say so plainly and leave the choice entirely to you.
