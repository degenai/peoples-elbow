Good To Know

Questions, answered

Is this a membership?
No. You book and pay per session. There is no monthly fee, no rollover credits to lose, and nothing to cancel.
Do you really not upsell?
Correct. You will not be pitched add-ons mid-session or pushed into a package at checkout. If a longer or more frequent session would help, we will say so plainly and leave the choice to you.
What kind of massage do you do?
Clinical and therapeutic work: deep tissue, trigger point, myofascial release, and relaxation when that is what the day calls for. Seated chair massage is available on-site for teams and events.
Where are you located?
Woodstock, GA, working out of trusted partner locations across Cherokee and Cobb counties. See the locations page for current sites and hours.
